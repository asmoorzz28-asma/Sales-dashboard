# 📈 Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer segments, and product categories through dynamic visualizations and business intelligence insights.



## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Sales Analytics Dashboard is a modern and interactive Power BI report developed to help businesses monitor sales performance and profitability across different customer segments, product sub-categories, and order dates. The dashboard provides valuable insights into revenue generation, profit distribution, and sales trends, enabling decision-makers to identify high-performing products, profitable customer segments, and seasonal sales patterns. It serves as an effective business intelligence solution for managers, analysts, and stakeholders who need real-time sales performance monitoring and data-driven decision-making.



## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main platform used for building interactive dashboards and reports.
- 🔄 **Power Query** – Used for importing, cleaning, and transforming raw sales data.
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for creating calculated measures such as Total Sales and Total Profit.
- 🗂 **Data Modeling** – Relationships between sales tables were established to enable accurate filtering and analysis.
- 📈 **Power BI Visualizations** – Used to create bar charts, donut charts, KPI cards, and time-series graphs.
- 🎨 **Custom Dashboard Theme** – Dark theme with blue accents for better readability and professional presentation.



## 4. Dashboard Features

### 📌 KPI Cards

- Total Sales
- Total Profit

These cards provide an instant overview of the overall business performance.



### 📊 Sales by Sub-Category

Displays sales generated by different product sub-categories including:

- Phones
- Chairs
- Storage
- Tables
- Binders
- Machines
- Accessories
- Copiers
- Bookcases
- Appliances
- Furnishings
- Paper
- Supplies
- Art
- Envelopes
- Labels
- Fasteners

This visualization helps identify the best-selling and least-performing product categories.


### 👥 Sales by Segment

Shows total sales distribution among customer segments:

- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

This chart helps understand which customer segment contributes the highest revenue.


### 💰 Profit by Segment

Displays profit contribution from each customer segment using a pie chart, allowing quick comparison of profitability.



### 📅 Sales Trend Over Time

A time-series chart that displays sales across different order dates.

Insights include:

- Sales growth trends
- Seasonal demand
- Sales spikes
- Business performance over multiple years



### 📉 Profit Trend Over Time

Tracks profit fluctuations across different order dates.

Useful for identifying:

- High-profit periods
- Loss-making transactions
- Profit volatility
- Business growth trends



## 5. Dataset Information

The dashboard is built using a retail sales dataset containing transactional sales information.

The dataset includes attributes such as:

- Order Date
- Product Category
- Product Sub-Category
- Customer Segment
- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity
- Order Details

The data was cleaned and transformed before visualization to ensure accuracy and consistency.

- Which product sub-category generates the highest sales?
- Which customer segment contributes the highest revenue?
- Which segment is the most profitable?
- How have sales changed over time?
- What are the overall sales and profit figures?
- Which products require improvement?
- Are there seasonal patterns in sales and profit?
